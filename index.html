what?   announcing a new edition of inérciaDemoparty2005, the best (and only) Portuguese demoparty!
     
when & where   6-12 august 2018
caparica, portugal (closest airport: Lisbon (LIS))
     
event   - invite only (email jeenio or ps to get your name on the list)
- small villa with chairs, electricity, wifi, projector, sound system, and a nice garden to lounge and camp at
- 4 bunk beds, 1 sofa couples bed, 1 singles sofa bed, lots of floor and space to camp
- 5 minutes drive from the ocean
- whole week of demomaking residency (limited to 12 visitors)
- compos on friday night (limited to 36 visitors)
- priority queue to foreign sceners coming for the full length
- entrance fee 5 euros (includes 3 beers and/or soda)
- meals available on site (10 euros per meal)

- 11 saturday: random socializing; democompo night
- 12 sunday: grab your shit and get out
